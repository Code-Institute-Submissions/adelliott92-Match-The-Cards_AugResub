HOW TO PLAY MATCH THE CARDS!

The aim of the game is to find all 6 matches by clicking on the cards until you find a match and see if you can beat your top score or find all the matches in one shot without getting any matches wrong.

Scoring System

Find a match and you will earn 5 points.

Get match wrong and you lose 2 points from your current score.
